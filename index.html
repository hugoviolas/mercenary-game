How To Play

  • ↑ / A : move up
  • ↓ / S : move down
  • ← / Q : move left
  • → / D : move right
  • Space Bar : sword hit

Avoid small skeletons arrows and big skeletons hits, survive the 5 waves to win the game

Mercenary

You are a mercenary employed by Prince Haroun. The skeletons are attacking and you are in the front line to defend the main gate of the castle... Don't get hit by the skeletons or their arrows, and don't let them pass or you will lose lives !
